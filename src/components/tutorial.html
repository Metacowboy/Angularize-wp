Insert Featured Image

Click this area to insert a featured image for your post. You can also drag and drop an image in here.

TIP: Featured images ARE REQUIRED for all posts

Enter Story Title

To continue, enter the title to your story here.

TIP: A catchy title ATTRACTS readers to your story!

Enter Story Excerpt

Next, enter the excerpt to your story. The excerpt is one of the first things visitors see on your post.

TIP: An interesting and thought-provoking excerpt draws readers to your post! Also it must be at least 2 lines!

Your location

Next, you will enter your location in here. Let our readers know where you are telling this story from!

TIP: You can either allow us to determine your location when you visit the new post page or enter your location manually.

Start telling your story

Finally! Start telling your story in this space.

TIP: To insert an image within your story, simply drag and drop the image into this space!

Formatting toolbar

The toolbar provides more options to the storyteller! Create blockquotes, lists, strikethroughs, bold, italicize and underline text, add links and more!

TIP: The toolbar is available upon clicking the 'Write Your Story Here" area

Select Category & Add Tags

Select from our site's categories here. Also add tags to your story here as well!

TIP: Tags help make your stories more discoverable!
